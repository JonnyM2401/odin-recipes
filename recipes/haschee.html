Haschee

A German ground beef tomato sauce usually served over noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1 TBSP olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 8 ounces ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 TBSP prepared mustard
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • salt & freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 cups plus 1 TBSP water
  • 1 TBSP cornstarch
  • spaetzle or noodles for serving

Preparation

  1. Heat the oil in a large saute pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the diced onion and tomato to the pan and cook, stirring occasionally, until softened, about 8 minutes.
  3. Add the ground beef to the pan and cook, while breaking up the beef, until lightly browned, about 6 minutes.
  4. Stir in the mustard, paprika, 1 tsp salt, 1/2 tsp black pepper, and 2 cups of the water and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer, stirring occasionally, for about 30 minutes.
  6. In a small bowl mix together the cornstarch and 1 TBSP water until the cornstarch has dissolved.
  7. Stir the cornstarch mixture in with the beef sauce until well combined.
  8. Continue to simmer the sauce over medium-low heat, stirring often, until thickened, about 10 more minutes.
  9. Season the sauce with salt & pepper to taste then serve over spaetzle or noodles.
